+ - About Company

Lian Kavan Health Tourism

Lian Kavan Health Tourism international Company with a long presence on various areas, but mostly Medical Tourism, was established in 2010. In addition to its experienced managers, it is known throughout the country as the first company in the field of Health Tourism industry which is authorized by the organization of Cultural Heritage, Handicrafts and Tourism of the Islamic Republic Of Iran. The first branch office was established in Iraq in 2011, by employing particular management solutions in providing high-quality healthcare services to patients, the company started its new branches’ activity in UAE and Afghanistan in 2012, Oman in 2013 and recently in 2015 in Kazakhstan. In 2017, at the first National Symposium on the Celebration of health tourism Top Brands in Iran, Lian Kavan has been announced as the top brand of the country. Moreover, with Constantly attending national and international exhibitions, it has achieved a broad understanding of health tourism industry. Lian Kavan’s main purpose is to offer medical tourism services based on various aspects in scientific specializations, providing the most recent medical equipment, employing professional and experienced physicians and managers to satisfied clients from start to finish. According to the Importance of Customer Satisfaction, Lian kavan- a community of health managers’, Physicians, Tourism managers, researchers, hospital administrators and business professionals, can provide a master plan for health travelers from middle-east area and other countries. Choosing the proper health care center and doctors, Lian kavan will offer a high level of healthcare and tourism services with the best quality tailored to the patient financial ability such as Visa application, flight tickets, accommodations and … around the world and Certainly, from the beginning to the end of the process, the patient will be accompanied by our team. Providing information of the top physicians and hospitals around the world, Lian Kavan makes it possible for all patients to choose their appropriate health center in any country. Furthermore, Due to the removal of brokers, and the direct contact with health centers and doctors, Lian Kavan is able to offer the best prices and health services in middle east and other countries. The advantages of Lian Kavan Health tourism services – Employing experienced managers and executive staffs from the beginning to the end – Low-cost health tourism packages rather than those who directly refer to health centers in person – Domestic and International services all around the world – Cooperating with the best travel agents, residential centers, hotels and airlines – Applying IT infrastructure and Telemedicine services in health tourism Lian Kavan Services – Free consultation to diagnose patient’s diseases (health problems) and to choose the appropriate doctors and medical centers. – Arranging the patient’s treatment at medical centers with flexible and tailor-made packages. – Organizing flights and accommodation for patients and his accompaniment. – Liaising patient with the doctors and accompanying with an interpreter speaking patient’s language during his stay. – Take advantage of sightseeing or shopping at patient’s destination.
